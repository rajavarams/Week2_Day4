My favorite restaurants

This site is all about restaurants I would like to recommend for vegetarian food.

California Pizza

Hand tossed pizzas with variety of toppings. Yummy appetizers. You can pick your own toppins. Greatly restaurant for families with kids.

Olive Garden

Best Italian food. If you are a vegetarian, you have lot of options from salada, soups, different pasta entrees, breadsticks and salad.

On The Border

Mouthwatering Mexican fajitas. All you can eat salsa, chips. Best burritos in town. They have many options for vegetarians and kids.
